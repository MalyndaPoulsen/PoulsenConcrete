Our Team

I am a registered nurse for the state of Idaho and the state of Oregon. I am also a fitness provider, I have years of personal training and teaching fitness classes and now focus most of my time working for home care and teaching yoga. I see almost daily the importance of providing excellent care for our aging loved ones in order to prevent family from getting burnt out. When my family was providing care for our loved ones, we questioned whether to hire out for the fear of the unknown. Will who cares for our loved ones be safe, trust worthy, and reliable? This is where the importance of hiring the best becomes essential. I personally interview each employee and hire out only if I feel they have these characteristics.

Kelly Young
